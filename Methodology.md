
## Overview

For this assignment, I chose to research companies in **Ahmedabad, Gujarat**, focusing on the following two segments:

 **Specialty Biotech**
 **Specialty Diagnostics & Life-Science Tools**

My goal was to identify companies that closely match the given criteria and evaluate them using the provided eligibility gates and scoring framework.

## Research Approach

I started by creating a broad list of companies operating in the selected segments. I then shortlisted them based on the two mandatory eligibility checks:

 **E1 (Producer):** The company should manufacture products or provide specialized services using its own facilities or operational infrastructure.
 **E2 (Accessible):** The company should have an operational presence in India.

After this initial filtering, I reviewed each shortlisted company against the remaining evaluation criteria, including differentiation, leadership quality, industry growth potential, evidence of expansion, operational maturity, and leadership structure.

Where exact information such as revenue or management details was not publicly available, I used the best available public sources and treated those values as estimates.

## Sources Used

The research was based on publicly available information, including:

* Official company websites
* Product and service pages
* Company profiles and public announcements
* Industry publications and business directories
* Professional networking platforms
* AI-assisted tools for organizing information and improving research efficiency

## Deliverables

This submission contains:

1. **Company Profiling Spreadsheet** – A structured list of shortlisted companies with eligibility checks, scoring, verdicts, and supporting notes.
2. **Methodology Document** – A summary of the research process, selection approach, and sources used during the assignment.

## Key Takeaways

Working on this assignment helped me understand that selecting the right company requires more than looking at its industry. Factors such as in-house manufacturing capability, technical specialization, operational maturity, and leadership structure play an important role in determining whether a company is a strong fit for the given criteria. It also highlighted the value of using multiple public sources and documenting assumptions when complete information is not available.
